Our Story

Where Beauty Meets Intention

At Diverse Hair Designs by Peachie, we believe every visit should feel like a personal retreat. Our approach is simple yet rare in today's fast-paced world — we dedicate our full attention to one client at a time.

From precision cuts to transformative color work, every service is performed with meticulous care and artistic vision. Your hair tells your story — we're here to make sure it's beautifully told.

Meet the Artist

Peachie Flower

Owner & Lead Stylist

Our salon is owned and operated by Peachie.

Peachie is a hairstylist that specializes in haircutting, coloring — such as balayage, highlights, ombré and color correction. She is certified in American Smoothing Treatment and Japanese Straightening with 14 years of experience in the hair industry. She graduated in 2010 with a degree in cosmetology at Paul Mitchell the School of Fayetteville.

Her passion for hairstyling stems from a deep desire to find joy in creativity while making a meaningful difference in people's lives. Hair is more than just hair — it is a part of our identity, culture, and life experience.
